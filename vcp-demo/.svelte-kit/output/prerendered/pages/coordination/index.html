What VCP Enables

Graduated Disclosure

Agents reveal interests incrementally based on trust and stakes. Position first, interests later, red lines only when necessary.

Strategic Privacy

Private valuations remain private. Agents signal intent without revealing exact numbers. Fair competition without exploitation.

Auditable Outcomes

Every decision has a clear audit trail. See what was shared, withheld, and how it influenced the final outcome.

The Core Insight

Multi-agent systems need the same privacy considerations humans do. Full transparency leads to exploitation; zero context leads to adversarial dynamics. VCP enables the right amount of mutual understanding—enough to cooperate, not enough to manipulate.
