The Core Insight

The same context that enables personalization can also enable exploitation. Psychosecurity flips the script: VCP context that protects rather than targets. Your vulnerability signals become shields, not vulnerabilities. Platforms that respect VCP work with your context to help; those that don't get less access, not more.

A Note on Sensitive Topics

The Mental Health Context demo discusses sensitive topics including crisis situations. All examples are simulated for educational purposes. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a crisis helpline in your region.
